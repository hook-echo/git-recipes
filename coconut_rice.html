Coconut Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a medium‑sized pot.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium‑high heat.
  3. Reduce to a gentle simmer, cover the pot, and cook for about 20 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, fluff the rice with a fork, and let it sit covered for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
Tip: For extra aroma, toast the shredded coconut lightly in a dry pan before adding it.
